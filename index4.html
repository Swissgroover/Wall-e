ABOUT THE FILM

Wall-e is a computer-animated science fiction movie produced by pixar animation studio on 2008. Wall-e was released in US on june 2008, it grossed 533mil worldwide and winning the 2008 golden globe for best animated film. Wall-e was the most complex production because of the world and history that had to be conveyed. Whereas most pixard products have 75.000 storyboardsm Wall-e had 125.000. Pixar studied chernobyl and city of sofia to crate a perfeckt ruined world.
